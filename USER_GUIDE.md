## Getting Started

## Creating Your First Proposal

### Step 1: Upload Client Brief

1. Click **"New Proposal"** from the dashboard
2. Upload your client's brief in one of these formats:
   - Text (.txt)
   - Word (.docx)
   - PDF (.pdf)
   - Or paste the brief directly

3. Click **"Analyze Brief"**

### Step 2: Review AI Analysis

ProposalPilot's AI will analyze the brief and provide:

- **Project Summary**: Overview of client needs
- **Key Requirements**: Extracted deliverables and scope
- **Budget Insights**: Estimated project value and pricing suggestions
- **Timeline Estimate**: Suggested project duration
- **Risk Assessment**: Potential challenges and considerations

**What to look for:**
- Verify the AI correctly understood the client's needs
- Check that all key requirements are captured
- Review budget recommendations

### Step 3: Generate Proposal

1. Click **"Generate Proposal"**
2. AI creates a professional proposal with:
   - Executive summary
   - Scope of work
   - **3-tier pricing** (Good/Better/Best)
   - Timeline and milestones
   - Terms and conditions

3. Review the generated proposal

### Step 4: Customize Your Proposal

Use the **Rich Text Editor** to:

- **Edit content**: Click any section to edit
- **Format text**: Bold, italic, headings, bullet points
- **Add visuals**: Insert images, tables, charts
- **Adjust pricing**: Modify the 3-tier pricing structure
- **Customize sections**: Add/remove sections as needed

**Pro Tips:**
- Keep the executive summary concise (2-3 paragraphs)
- Use bullet points for deliverables
- Include client-specific examples in case studies
- Add visual elements to break up text

### Step 5: Quality Check

1. Click the **"Quality Score"** button in the sidebar
2. Review your proposal's score (0-100)
3. See actionable suggestions:
   - ‚úì Strengths
   - ‚ö†Ô∏è Areas for improvement
   - üí° Recommendations

4. Apply suggestions to improve your score

### Step 6: Share with Client

**Option 1: Public Link**
1. Click **"Share"**
2. Copy the generated link
3. Send to your client via email

**Option 2: Export**
1. Click **"Export"**
2. Choose format:
   - **PDF**: Professional, print-ready
   - **DOCX**: Editable Word document
3. Download and send to client

## Client Engagement Tracking

### Understanding Engagement Metrics

When you share a proposal via public link, ProposalPilot tracks:

1. **Views**: How many times the proposal was opened
2. **Time Spent**: Total time client spent reviewing
3. **Section Engagement**: Which sections got the most attention
4. **Last Viewed**: When the client last accessed the proposal

### Engagement Dashboard

Access engagement data:
1. Go to your proposal list
2. Click **"Analytics"** on any shared proposal
3. View detailed metrics:
   - View count and unique visitors
   - Time spent per section
   - Heatmap of client interest
   - View history timeline

### Interpreting Engagement Data

**High engagement indicators:**
- ‚úÖ Multiple views
- ‚úÖ Long time spent on pricing section
- ‚úÖ Recent views

**Low engagement indicators:**
- ‚ùå No views after 48 hours
- ‚ùå Very short time spent
- ‚ùå Pricing section not viewed

**Action Tips:**
- **No views**: Send a follow-up email
- **Short time**: Simplify your proposal
- **Multiple views**: Client is interested - follow up!

### Automated Follow-ups

Enable automatic email reminders:
1. Go to Settings > Notifications
2. Enable "Engagement Alerts"
3. Set follow-up timing:
   - After 3 days if not viewed
   - After 7 days if no response
4. Customize email templates

## Best Practices

### Writing Effective Briefs

For best AI analysis results:

‚úÖ **DO:**
- Include clear project objectives
- Specify deliverables explicitly
- Mention budget range or constraints
- State timeline expectations
- Include client company background

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Use vague language
- Omit key requirements
- Submit extremely short briefs (< 100 words)
- Include unrelated information

### Optimizing Proposal Quality

**Content Tips:**
1. **Executive Summary**: Lead with client benefits
2. **Scope**: Be specific but not overwhelming
3. **Pricing**: Explain value differences between tiers
4. **Timeline**: Show realistic milestones
5. **Case Studies**: Include relevant examples

**Design Tips:**
1. Use headers to break up content
2. Add visuals (charts, mockups, photos)
3. Keep paragraphs short (3-4 lines)
4. Use bullet points for lists
5. Maintain consistent formatting

### Following Up with Clients

**Timing Guidelines:**
- **24-48 hours**: Initial follow-up after sending
- **3-5 days**: If no engagement shown
- **7 days**: Final follow-up before archiving

**Email Template:**
```
Subject: Quick question about the proposal

Hi [Client Name],

I wanted to follow up on the proposal I sent over on [date].

I noticed you viewed the [section name] - did you have any questions about [specific aspect]?

I'm happy to hop on a quick call to discuss any details.

Looking forward to hearing from you!

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Pricing Strategy

**3-Tier Pricing Tips:**

1. **Good Tier**: Entry point with core deliverables
2. **Better Tier**: Most popular - add 1-2 valuable features
3. **Best Tier**: Premium with all extras

**Pricing Psychology:**
- Price the middle tier as your target
- Make the difference between tiers meaningful
- Anchor with the highest price
- Show value, not just features

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: How does the AI generate proposals?**
A: ProposalPilot uses Claude AI by Anthropic to analyze your brief, understand requirements, and generate professional proposal content based on industry best practices.

**Q: Can I customize the AI-generated content?**
A: Yes! All generated content is fully editable. Use our rich text editor to customize any section.

**Q: How accurate is the brief analysis?**
A: The AI is highly accurate but should be reviewed. Always verify that key requirements are correctly captured.

**Q: What languages are supported?**
A: Currently, ProposalPilot supports English. More languages coming soon.

### Pricing & Billing

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

**Q: Do unused proposals roll over?**
A: No, proposal limits reset each month. However, all created proposals remain accessible.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept all major credit cards via Stripe (Visa, MasterCard, Amex, Discover).

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) and secure HTTPS connections. Data is stored on Azure with enterprise-grade security.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, you can export individual proposals as PDF/DOCX and analytics data as CSV/Excel.

**Q: Do you have an API?**
A: API access is available on Enterprise plans. Contact support for API documentation.

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: ProposalPilot works on all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). We recommend using the latest version.

### Troubleshooting

**Q: The AI analysis seems incorrect. What should I do?**
A: Try rephrasing the brief with clearer requirements. You can also regenerate the analysis or contact support for help.

**Q: My client can't access the shared link.**
A: Check that:
- The link hasn't expired
- The proposal isn't archived
- There are no firewall restrictions on their end

**Q: The quality score is low. How do I improve it?**
A: Click on the quality score to see specific suggestions. Common improvements include:
- Adding more detail to deliverables
- Strengthening the executive summary
- Including case studies or examples
- Improving pricing justification

**Q: I'm getting a "proposal limit reached" error.**
A: You've hit your monthly limit. Either upgrade your plan or wait until next month when limits reset.
